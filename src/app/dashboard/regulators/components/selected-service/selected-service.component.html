Horticultural Crops Directorate (HCD)

Services

  1. To enhance compliance with standards and other market access requirements so as to ensure conformity to quality and quantity standards required by the markets
  2. To review and develop product standards and codes of practice in collaboration with Kenya Bureau of Standards ( KEBS)
  3. To ensure registration and licensing of industry players
  4. To promote self-regulation in line with relevant regulations.

Requirements

  1. List of shareholders (Form CR-12)
  2. Certificate of incorporation
  3. Company PIN certificate
  4. Valid business permit
  5. Ordinary Membership Certificate

Costs

  1. Export Licence: KES 15,000
  2. Export Licence Renewal: KES 10,000
